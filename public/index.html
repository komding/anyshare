Welcome to the Text Sharing Website

Ever had a hard time sharing some sort of text from your smartphone to pc or from pc to other pc. There you go...!


This website allows you to share text in just two click.
1. Go to Publish Text.
2. Enter the text you want to share.
3. Enter the Unique Identifier (may be your name or phone mumber).
4. Click publish.
Now you can type the link in the browser of the pc or phone you want to share the text
or just open our website goto Access Text and enter Unique Identifier and copy the shared text.


Your text remains for 15 minutes before it get vanishes from the database.
Start sharing your text now!


we will be adding more features to the website in future. You would be able to share live text, any pdf, ppt, docx etc.. files easily.
